Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015 with just 2 computers in a small office, Petra ICT Hub has grown to become Benue's leading digital skills training center. What began as a passion project to bridge the digital divide has transformed into a thriving community hub.

2015
Founded in Makurdi

Started with basic computer literacy classes for 12 students

2018
Expanded Course Offerings

Added programming and design courses in response to demand

2021
New Facility Opened

Moved to our current location with 30 workstations

2023
1000+ Graduates

Celebrated our 1000th graduate with industry partnerships
